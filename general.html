Real Madrid boycotts 2024 Ballon d'Or after Rodri's win over Vinicius Jr.

The 2024 Ballon d'Or choice of Manchester City's Rodri over Real Madrid's Vinicius Junior has stirred controversy. Despite Vinicius’s 26 goals and 11 assists, Real Madrid seems discontent, viewing the decision as politically influenced. Many argue Rodri's contributions fall short of Vinicius's on-field impact, raising questions about the award's criteria and impartiality.
