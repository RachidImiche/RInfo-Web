TikTok US Ban: Why the Clock Is Ticking

The U.S. government is pushing for ByteDance to sell TikTok's U.S. operations by January 19, 2025, citing national security concerns over its Chinese ownership. If ByteDance refuses, TikTok could be banned, affecting millions of users. The company is fighting back legally, claiming the ban violates First Amendment rights, while the outcome could influence future regulations on foreign-owned digital platforms
